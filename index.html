The KiwiLander Format

In the tradition of the Australian and Canadian Highlander formats, KiwiLander is our new community designed local casual singleton format. KiwiLander decks consist of 60 cards with unique names (excluding basic lands) and are played in a classic one-on-one match of knock-down drag-out Magic the Gathering with no sideboard. All vintage legal cards excluding those on the reserved list are fair game, but with a twist: a handful of powerful cards are assigned a value on our points list.

Each deck has a 5 point limit - so choose wisely!
