The Kiwilander Format

  • Your deck must consist of at least 60 cards, with no sideboard.
  • Your deck must contain no more than a total of 5 points.
  • After game 1, present your deck to your opponent for a points check.
  • All cards not legal for vintage play are banned.
  • Additionally, all cards on the official reserved list are banned.
  • Decks may contain no cards with the same name except for basic lands, or cards that explicitly state otherwise (e.g. Relentless Rats).
